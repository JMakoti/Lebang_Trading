About Us

Lebang Trading cc commenced operation in 2004 and exhibited steady growth since then. It is a dynamic and vibrant building, construction and civil engineering Company established after much research and thought. Recognizing the shortcomings and pitfalls in the engineering and construction sector, an endeavour was made to address these shortcomings.

Our strategy is made up of input from past and present clients, our agents, ourselves and other business associates. Its intention is to assist the country with dealing with all construction Related Services. It caters for the needs of individuals, investors, entrepreneurs and corporate clientele.
